AI-Powered Plant Disease Detection

Our system uses a state-of-the-art deep learning model to accurately identify a wide range of plant diseases from images. Simply upload a photo of a plant leaf, and our AI will analyze it in real-time to provide a diagnosis.

Disease Tracking and History

Keep a record of all your past disease detections. Our disease tracker allows you to view a history of your scans, including the plant type, detected disease, and confidence score. This helps in monitoring the health of your crops over time.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your crop health data with our interactive analytics dashboard. See charts and statistics on disease distribution, the number of scans performed, and the types of plants being monitored. Gain valuable insights to make informed decisions.

Smart Treatment Recommendations

For each detected disease, our system provides a set of smart treatment recommendations. These include chemical, biological, and preventive measures to help you effectively manage and treat the identified plant diseases.

PDF Report Generation

Generate a detailed PDF report for each disease diagnosis. The report includes the uploaded image, the prediction result, confidence score, and treatment recommendations. This is useful for record-keeping and sharing with experts.
