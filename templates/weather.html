    How weather drives disease spread

    • Humidity ≥ 85% and 18–30°C accelerates fungal diseases (mildew, blight, rust).
    • Rain and leaf wetness increase splash-borne bacterial and foliar pathogen spread.
    • Wind ≥ 20 kph aids airborne spore movement and increases vector activity.
    • Hot & dry can lower fungal pressure but raise mite/virus-vector risks.

    Always follow local regulations and product labels when applying plant protection products.
